# Product Ideas

## Idea 1 — Bee Performance Dashboard
A real-time dashboard showing each bee's performance metrics — tasks completed, honey spent, error rate, average response time. Org admins can see which bees are performing well and which need tuning. Could include a weekly digest email.

## Idea 2 — Knowledge Graph Import from Notion
Allow users to import their existing Notion pages directly into the Hive Mind as context nodes. Each Notion page becomes a neuron. Tags and properties map to node metadata automatically.

## Idea 3 — Bee Templates Marketplace
A marketplace where users can share and discover pre-built bee configurations. A "Sales Bee" template, a "Customer Support Bee" template etc. One-click install into your org. Could be free or paid.

## Idea 4 — Mobile App for Approvals
A lightweight mobile app specifically for approval gates. When a bee reaches a human approval gate, the approver gets a push notification, can review the bee's plan, and approve or reject from their phone in one tap.

## Idea 5 — Scheduled Knowledge Reviews
Automatically surface knowledge nodes that haven't been reviewed in 90+ days and prompt the org to confirm they are still accurate. Prevents the Hive Mind from going stale over time.
